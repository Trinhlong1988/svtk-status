### Round 3 — Spawn position collision R75

**Bug:** 284 cặp NPC có spawn position cách < 8 tile trong cùng sceneId. Vi phạm R75 `MIN_NPC_SPACING = 8`.

**Reproducible test:**
```python
# Example: sceneId=4203, idx=2 at (50,50), idx=8 at (50,50), distance=0
```

**Fix:**
- `pick_collision_free_pos(scene_id, rng, tracker)`: cell-grid với cell size = MIN_NPC_SPACING (8 tile)
- Center-of-cell deterministic — adjacent cells exactly MIN_SPACING apart
- Tracker seeded với 3×3 neighborhood của mỗi existing position → reserve buffer
- Generated-vs-generated AND existing-vs-generated = 0 close pairs

**Post-fix:** gen-gen=0, ex-gen=0, ex-ex=284 (alert LEAD MED, source immutable)

### Round 4 — class_hierarchy field R80

**Bug:** 0/10000 NPC có `class_hierarchy` field. R80 yêu cầu 6 mức.

**Fix:** Function `class_hierarchy_for(tier, npc_type)`:
- Boss + tier 9 → "than"
- Boss + tier 7-8 → "thanh"
- Boss + tier 6 → "boss"
- Boss + tier 5 → "mini_boss"
- Others: tier 9→than, 7-8→thanh, 6→boss, 5→mini_boss, 3-4→elite, 0-2→regular

**Distribution post-fix:** regular=5962, elite=2588, mini_boss=671, boss=443, thanh=318, than=18
